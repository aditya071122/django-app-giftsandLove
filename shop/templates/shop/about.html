Hi, I’m Shikha Savita

Founder of GiftsandLove | BBA Student | Creative Entrepreneur

My Story

I started GiftsandLove with a simple dream — to bring happiness through thoughtful and meaningful gifts. Being a BBA student, I always believed in combining creativity with passion, and that’s how this small business began. What started as an Instagram page has now grown into a loving community of customers who value handmade and hand-picked gifts.

What We Offer

At GiftsandLove, every item is selected or crafted with care. Whether it's birthdays, anniversaries, festive gifting, or a simple surprise — we make sure your gift feels special. I aim to provide unique products that express emotion and create memories.

Why GiftsandLove?

Gifts are not just items — they are feelings wrapped with love. Through this small business, I want to help you celebrate your relationships with beautiful, meaningful, and memorable gifts. Your love and support motivate me to continue creating better experiences each day.
